
Question: A solution of 20 L of wine and water contains 80% wine. How much water (in L) must be added to this solution in order to make it a 40% wine solution?
A: 10 L
B: 20 L
C: 15 L
D: 25 L
Answer: B
Explanation: In 20 L solution, wine = 20 × 80% = 16 L and water = 4 L. To make wine 40%, total solution must be 16/0.4 = 40 L. Thus, 40 − 20 = 20 L of water must be added.

Question: 7 people sit around a circular table. What is the probability that 2 specific people are always next to each other?
A: 0.33
B: 0.1
C: 0.167
D: None of these
Answer: A
Explanation: Total circular arrangements = 6! = 720. Treat 2 people as one unit: (6-1)! × 2! = 5! × 2 = 240. Probability = 240/720 = 1/3 ≈ 0.33.

Question: Out of 2n consecutive integers, (n+1) are selected at random. What is the probability their HCF is 1?
A: 1
B: 1/(n+1)
C: 1/2
D: 1/(n+2)
Answer: A
Explanation: By Pigeonhole Principle, selecting n+1 integers from 2n consecutive numbers guarantees at least two are consecutive. HCF of any two consecutive integers = 1, so the overall HCF = 1 always. Probability = 1.
